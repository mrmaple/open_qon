<Ned>

<Ned> is a small grassroots group of people and orgs working together to make a better world.
<Ned> is a global collaboration and anyone can participate.

Ned's previous community was a Yahoo group which is still active.

Small actions, little changes, making a difference, regular everyday people from around the world working together in new and interesting ways to help make the world a little bit better place. We don't have to have the riches of Bill Gates or the fame of Bono to help create a better world, we simply just have the desire to want to help in whatever way we can.
Right here, right now.

Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful committed citizens can change the world.
